WageWorks & Uber Introduce
a Whole New Way to Commute

WageWorks and Uber are revolutionizing your commute, giving you more options on how to get to and from work.

We're excited to enter into a first-in-market partnership with Uber, the world’s leading rideshare company, to offer you the convenience of using your WageWorks® Commuter Benefit Account to pay for uberPOOL rides. This new partnership gives you the flexibility to use your pre-tax funds to pay for uberPOOL rides when you commute.

Now you can save an average of 30% when you rideshare to work via uberPOOL. That’s more money back in your pocket every month.

UberPOOL is currently available in Atlanta, Boston, Chicago, Denver, Los Angeles, Miami, New Jersey, New York City, Philadelphia, San Diego, San Francisco, Seattle and Washington D.C. Use of WageWorks commuter benefits will be phased in to these and any new markets in which uberPOOL is offered.

With the addition of uberPOOL you now have even more options for your daily commute!
